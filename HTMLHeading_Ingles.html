HTML Heading

HTML Heading are titles or subtitles that you want to display on a webpage.

Example:

Heading 1

Heading 2

Heading 3

Heading 4

Heading 5
Heading 6

HTML Headings

HTML Heading are defined with the <h1> to <h6>

tags.

<h1> defines the most important heading. <h6> defines the least important heading.

Example:

<h1>Heading 1<h1>
<h2>Heading 2<h2>
<h3>Heading 3<h3>
<h4>Heading 4<h4>
<h5>Heading 5<h5>
<h6>Heading 6<h6>
